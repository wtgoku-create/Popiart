# Doodle 模板：步骤流程图（Step-by-Step Flow）

适用：
- 教程步骤
- 操作流程
- 工作流
- 学习路径
- 从问题到解决的路径图

## 什么时候用

当内容是明显的“先后顺序”时用它：
- 先做什么
- 再做什么
- 最后怎么收尾

如果用户想表达的是“不同因素互相影响”，通常更适合**机制图**；如果是“两边差异”，通常更适合**对比图**。

## 推荐版式

### 版式 A：横向 4~6 步（默认）
- 从左到右
- 每步一个卡片
- 卡片内：步骤名 + 1 句动作 + 1 个小图标/小角色动作
- 步骤之间用箭头连接

### 版式 B：纵向流程
- 适合手机长图或需要更细解释的场景
- 但 16:9 横版默认优先横向

## 文案规则

- 4~6 步最稳
- 每步只写短句
- 步骤名尽量是动词开头
- 避免每步都塞很多解释小字

## Prompt 模板（可直接改）

```text
Hand-drawn doodle infographic on white paper, pencil sketch outlines, marker highlights, sticky notes, arrows, boxed modules, tape decoration, cute anthropomorphic mascot characters, playful but smart, high-information-density but clean composition, classroom board / notebook poster feeling, Chinese labels, educational editorial illustration, not childish, not photorealistic.

At the top center, a clear Chinese title: '[标题]'.

Create a left-to-right flowchart with [4/5/6] steps.
Each step is a boxed module with:
- a short Chinese step title
- one concise action sentence
- a small icon or mascot action
Connect the steps with clear arrows.
At the end, add a small final takeaway box.
Avoid long paragraphs.
```

## 示例：从拖延到开工的 5 步

```text
Hand-drawn doodle infographic on white paper, pencil sketch outlines, marker highlights, sticky notes, arrows, boxed modules, tape decoration, cute anthropomorphic mascot characters, playful but smart, high-information-density but clean composition, classroom board / notebook poster feeling, Chinese labels, educational editorial illustration.

At the top center, a clear Chinese title: '从拖延到开工的 5 步'.

Create a left-to-right flowchart with 5 steps.
Step 1: 看见任务 — 承认它存在.
Step 2: 缩小动作 — 只做最小一步.
Step 3: 限时开始 — 先做10分钟.
Step 4: 去掉完美主义 — 先出粗糙版.
Step 5: 留下惯性 — 做完后顺手安排下一步.
Use a cute snail mascot at the beginning and a cute lobster mascot near the action steps.
Add a small final takeaway box: '先动起来，比想明白更重要'.
```

## 小提示

- 想要更“教程感”：每步加编号圆点
- 想要更“可爱”：让角色在每一步做不同动作
- 想要更“办公友好”：步骤名短、字大、箭头明显
